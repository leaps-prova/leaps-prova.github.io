TheEye

Change the way you look at the magazines

Have you ever thought about what reading would have been like in Middle Age? Or whish to browse the literary magazines of the first newspapers printed in nineteenth-century cafes? Have you ever tried to imagine what newspapers will be like in a hundred years? We have. And here is the result.

TheEye is an original website that allows you to control the graphic desing of the updated articles. Thanks to its widgets and capabilities, you will be able to change the way modern articles are displayed, offering the possibility to choose among nearly a millennium of layout and graphics history.

In addition, it will allow you to access information about and within the texts themselves, making reading a new experience augmented by the power of computers.

Browse the site, choose the item that interests you most and try to change its graphics. Then, read them in a completely new way. Ready to get started?


How it works

TheEye is a website that allows you to change the layout of your favorite articles. Choose an historical (or maybe not) style and look how your reading looks like!



TheEye - The idea
