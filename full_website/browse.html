How it works

TheEye is a website that allows you to change the layout of your favorite articles. Choose an historical (or maybe not) style and look how your reading looks like!



TheEye - The idea

TheEye has been realized during the University of Bologna course in Information Modeling and Web Technologies held by Professor Fabio Vitali in the y.a. 2019-2020. The website is built and managed by four students attending the course.
